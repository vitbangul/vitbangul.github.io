Jaehyun Kim(b.1993) is Painter and Visual Artist based on the Netherlands.
Our species set off from the foothill of a mountain range that we knew nothing about, and we have never stopped climbing. Climbing so fast that within two years we don’t know where we are, certain things aren’t likely to be true, we don’t know what should be protected, what should be obscured. The world, smarting from self inflicted wounds of an unprecedented scale rushes forward. Echoed inside of each of Jaehyun’s paintings is this climbing, a mechanism of selection with its own inherent speed. But the speed of this is more careful. Abundance, regeneration, extraction, depletion, each element mirrors Nature’s adjustments. Evolution does not look forward, but it builds forward looking creatures. Creatures who build paintings while soaking them in stillness, paintings vulnerable in the fragile balance of their systems. On the fringes of each is a question spreading to the center: what is the speed of things?
